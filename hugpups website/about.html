About Us

The goal of HugPups, a dog therapy rental site, would be to provide individuals with easy access to trained therapy dogs for emotional support and stress relief. By connecting people with calming, affectionate dogs, the site aims to improve mental well-being, reduce anxiety, and foster positive emotional connections.
