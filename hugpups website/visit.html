Visit The Team

Here at HugPups Dog Rental, our focus is on providing professional therapy dog rentals that cater to improved emotional well-being, reduced stress, and developed mental health. Our trained and certified therapy dogs are available for one-on-one sessions, group events, and corporate settings. Whether you require a calming presence on a stressful day, support during a session of therapy, or interactive experiences that help boost morale, our dogs are here to help. Our services can be tailored to suit individuals, families, schools, hospitals, and businesses. Let our dogs comfort, bring joy, and provide emotional support to you and your community.
