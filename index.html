What is our goal:

  • Promote Emotional Well-being: Provide resources and services to help individuals improve mental health and emotional wellness through dog therapy.
  • Connect People with Certified Therapy Dogs: Facilitate easy access to trained therapy dogs for individuals in need of comfort, stress relief, and companionship.
  • Educate on the Benefits of Dog Therapy: Offer information, articles, and research to raise awareness about the positive impact therapy dogs can have on various mental and physical health conditions.

Info about mental health and pet ownership:

Pets, especially therapy dogs, help reduce stress, anxiety, and depression by providing emotional support. Interacting with animals boosts mood and promotes relaxation through physical touch and companionship. This connection improves overall mental well-being and fosters emotional healing.
